About Us

At House-electronic, we believe that your home should be a place of comfort, efficiency, and innovation. That's why we handpick the best household appliances for you, combining high performance, modern design, and smart technology.

Since our launch, our mission has been simple: to make your daily life easier with reliable, affordable, and durable products. From the kitchen to the living room, the laundry room to the bedroom, we provide solutions tailored to every part of your home.

We are proud to offer responsive customer service, fast and free delivery on orders over €40, and hassle-free returns within 60 days. Join our community and enjoy exclusive offers by becoming a member for free.

Our Journey

The House-electronic journey began in 2019 with a simple yet ambitious idea: to revolutionize home equipment by combining technology, comfort, and affordability. In a constantly evolving market, we set out to offer a modern alternative for households seeking reliable, high-performance, and stylish products.

What started out as a small project quickly grew into a go-to platform for home technology enthusiasts. In just a few years, we expanded our catalog to meet the needs of every room in the house — from small appliances to the most innovative smart devices.

Today, House-electronic stands out as a trusted player in the industry, thanks to the dedication of our team, the loyalty of our customers, and our constant drive to innovate.
